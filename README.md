# Healthcare Statistics Portfolio

A portfolio of applied statistical analyses in healthcare using real-world and clinical datasets.

## Projects

### 1. Real-World Evidence Analysis: Type 2 Diabetes Risk Factors (NHANES 2017–2018)

Objective

Identify demographic, socioeconomic, and clinical factors associated with Type 2 Diabetes among U.S. adults using NHANES 2017–2018 data.

Methods

- Cohort construction and data cleaning
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Descriptive statistics
- Univariable logistic regression
- Multivariable logistic regression
- Sensitivity analysis
- Model diagnostics (VIF, Hosmer–Lemeshow test)

Key Findings

- Older age was associated with higher odds of diabetes.
- Higher BMI was associated with higher odds of diabetes.
- Hypertension showed the strongest clinical association with diabetes.
- Non-Hispanic Asian and Mexican American participants had higher adjusted odds of diabetes compared with Non-Hispanic White participants.
- Female participants had lower odds of diabetes.
